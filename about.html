BNL stands for Bonnavie Nathaniel Lopez which is the name of the owner and his wife. We have a total of 10 employees that manage our company. The business bids for the parts and sells it affordable to the buyer. The company collects and shipped auto car parts from Banaue Ifugao, Philippines and does deliveries nationwide. The common car parts that we have in our company are from a variety of car brands like Toyota, Mitsubishi, Honda, Suzuki, Hyundai and many more.
